Our Story

A Culinary Journey from
Pakistan to London

At Monal London, we bring you the soul of Pakistan’s culinary heritage, right here in the heart of the city. Named after the breathtaking Monal Valley, our restaurant is a tribute to the rich flavors, warm hospitality, and time-honored traditions that make Pakistani cuisine extraordinary.

Our menu is a celebration of authentic flavors, crafted with passion and precision. From the hearty Karahis slow-cooked to perfection to the charcoal-grilled BBQ and silky-smooth biryanis, every dish reflects the true essence of Pakistani food culture. Indulge in our signature tandoori delights, sizzling kebabs, and fragrant curries, all prepared using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you’re craving the comfort of home-style daals, the bold spices of a Shinwari Karahi, or the rich indulgence of a Monal Special Grill, we promise a culinary experience that feels like a journey through Pakistan.

Join us at Monal London, where every meal is a memory, and every bite tells a story of home.
